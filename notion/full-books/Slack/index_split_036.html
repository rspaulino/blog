Business Instead of Busyness

 

In which the author cuts to the chase and describes
the benefits of sensibly designed-in slack

 

THIS BOOK is divided about half and half between prescriptive and proscriptive material; half do’s and half don’ts. By time-honored convention, most of the don’ts come first.

Since the early going is somewhat don’t-intensive, I interrupt the plan with this chapter to give you a glimpse of what the results are likely to be if you do all or most of the do’s and don’t do many of the don’ts.
