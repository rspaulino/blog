Lines of Hierarchy and Lines of Leadership

 

Leadership is not restricted to acting only downward along the lines of organizational authority. The bread-and-butter acts of leadership that make companies healthy involve people leading their bosses, leading their peers, leading those in peer organizations, negotiating, mediating, and influencing, all without ever being granted the official power to do what they’re doing.

It’s enrolling someone who is distinctly outside the scope of your official power base that constitutes real leadership.
