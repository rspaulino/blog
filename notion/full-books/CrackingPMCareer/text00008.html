Product skills are the foundation for designing high-quality products that delight users and solve people's needs. In this section, we'll develop the skills for making better product decisions.

  • Chapter 4 : User Insight will teach us how to understand what people need and the problems they want a product to solve. We'll learn how to gather information from users and dig deeper to surface key insights. Finally, we'll explore the many types of user research.
  • Chapter 5 : Data Insight will teach us how to review data, analyze it, and use it for better decision-making. We'll talk about how to look at company metrics, and explore A/B testing and statistics.
  • Chapter 6 : Analytical Problem Solving will provide frameworks for making better decisions. We'll cover topics like systems thinking and learn techniques for tackling complex problems.
  • Chapter 7 : Product and Design Skills will develop our product mindset and our ability to drive product decisions. We'll learn about prototyping and brainstorming, and how to prioritize product decisions.
