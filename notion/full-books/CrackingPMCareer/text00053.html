There is no one path into or out of product management. The needed skills are too diverse for any single undergraduate degree or prior role to fully prepare you. Likewise, the skills you acquire are applicable to so many professions.

In this section, we've selected a handful of exceptional product leaders and charted their winding career paths and words of wisdom. You'll hear from:

  • Dylan Casey , a professional-cyclist-turned-product-leader, who will share how he transforms teams.
  • Brian Ellin , a climate activist, who turned his passion for the environment into a job.
  • Osi Imeokparia , a technology industry veteran, who brought political and social change.
  • Bangaly Kaba , an entrepreneur who grew some of the most successful social media networks in the world.
  • Sara Mauskopf , a founder and CEO, who will share how she started her own company.
  • Ken Norton , a product leader and entrepreneur, who will share his thoughts on careers in VC.
  • Anuj Rathi , a VP of product at India's largest food delivery marketplace, who is inspiring the next generation of Indian PMs.
  • Sachin Rekhi , a founder and CEO, who will share his approach for finding product-market fit.
  • Teresa Torres , a product coach, who will share why she moved from CEO to product coach.
  • Oji Udezue , a product leader and angel investor, who will share how he evaluates his career growth.
  • April Underwood , the former CPO of Slack, who will share how she grew into the CPO role and why a launch is more than shipping code to production.

We hope that you enjoy their thoughts as much as we do.
