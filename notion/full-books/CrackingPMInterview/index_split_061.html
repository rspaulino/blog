Cover Letters

Chapter 9

Cover letters are a mixed bag. Some companies care about them a lot, some companies don’t want them at all, and a lot of companies ask for them but don’t put much emphasis on them.

A well-written cover letter can help connect your background with the job requirements. This becomes particularly important if you don’t have the “ideal” background. It can be your way to show the reader that, although it doesn’t look like it at first glance, you have the right background.
