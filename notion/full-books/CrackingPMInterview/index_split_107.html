Who Needs To Code

Generally, the more recently you’ve coded, the more likely you are to be asked coding questions. Companies will often expect recent computer science graduates and current developers to code during their interviews.

Never coded before? You’re probably off the hook for the coding aspects, but you still might be expected to explain an approach to solve a problem. It’s worth reading this section, just in case. Some of the terms might be beyond your knowledge, but you’ll still benefit from understanding how to approach these questions.
