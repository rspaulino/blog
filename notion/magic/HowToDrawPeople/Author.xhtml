About the Author

Jeff Mellem is a professional artist and graphic designer. He earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts from the California State University, Fullerton, and studied at the American Animation Institute in North Hollywood, California. His previous book, Sketching People: Life Drawing Basics, was released by North Light Books in 2009, and he was one of the artists featured in Sketchbook Confidential: Secrets From the Private Sketches of Over 40 Master Artists.

For more on his work, visit www.jeffmellem.com.
