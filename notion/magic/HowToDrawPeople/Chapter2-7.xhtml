Hand

The hand can be complicated to draw because the fingers move in so many different ways. To simplify the hand, you can think of it like a mitten and treat the fingers as one solid mass.

1 Draw the Palm

Start by drawing the palm as a flattened box shape that tapers out from the wrist and peaks in the middle.

2 Draw the Fingers and Thumb

Attach a similarly sized shape to the front of the palm that tapers in for the fingers. You can angle or bend the finger mass as desired. Add a thumb shape off the side of the palm.
